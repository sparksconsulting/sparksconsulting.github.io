Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a profit assessment take?

Most assessments take 1-2 weeks depending on the complexity of your business and data. We'll provide a timeline during your initial consultation.

What if my data is really messy?

That's exactly what we specialize in! Messy, disorganized data is our starting point. We clean, organize, and structure it so it becomes reliable and actionable.

Do I need any special software or tools?

No. We work with the tools you already have (Excel, Google Sheets, etc.) and can recommend additional solutions only if they make sense for your business.

How much does this cost?

Projects vary based on complexity, but we offer a free consultation and audit to understand your needs before discussing pricing. There's no obligation.

Will I be able to maintain the dashboards myself?

Yes! We design everything to be simple and sustainable. We'll train you so you can maintain and update your dashboards independently.

Do you work with my industry?

We work with small businesses across many industries: retail, e-commerce, manufacturing, services, and more. We adapt our approach to your specific business model.
