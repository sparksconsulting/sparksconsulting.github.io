Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 27, 2026

Sparks Consulting Group ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit sparksconsultinggroup.com (the "Website") or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, or other information you provide when filling out forms or booking consultations.
  • Usage Information: Information automatically collected when you visit the Website, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referral URLs.
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve your experience and analyze Website traffic.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide services
  • Schedule consultations or follow up on bookings
  • Improve the Website and user experience
  • Send promotional or informational emails (you can opt out at any time)
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Service providers who assist us with Website hosting, email, or analytics
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect our rights

4. Your Choices

  • You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some Website features may not work properly.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Website is not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
